A. Full Dark + Glass

Dark backgrounds everywhere with frosted teal glass cards. Consistent dark theme across all pages. Prices and accents glow in cyan. High contrast, very immersive.

Trade-off: Images (hotel photos, destinations) need careful treatment on dark bg

B. Light + Teal Tint

Light warm-gray page background, white cards with subtle teal-tinted borders and shadows. Teal gradient icon containers. Bright, clean, but with brand personality through tinting.

Trade-off: Less dramatic, but great for readability and data-heavy pages

C. Hybrid -- White Cards on Dark

Dark page backgrounds for immersion, but cards themselves are white with strong shadows. Teal gradient icon badges. Best of both: dark drama + readable cards. Images look great.

Trade-off: Needs careful balance so cards don't feel "floating in void"
